What is Olivia Rae Designs?


Olivia Rae Designs (ORD) is a new fashion brand created by St. Louis native Olivia Rae. The brand was formed after designing custom skirts for her friends in high school-inspiring Olivia Rae to grow the company. While in high school, Olivia’s designs showcased collections at various fashion shows including Mathew Dickies Boys and Girls club, where she was the first youth designer to present. She encourages female entrepreneurship while defying typical fashion expectation by incorporating the sprit and flare of the modern women. The unique prints, textures, and seam work make the garments irresistible. Olivia Rae Designs premieres its first spring and summer collection in 2018. These collections are inspired by fun-loving, career oriented, modern women, demonstrated through the versatility in color and style to compliment multiple settings and occasions.

Meet Olivia Rae

At age 14 Olivia Rae noticed the impact appearance had on perception and success, therefore, she became the ‘equalizer’-- leveling the playing field for opportunities through fashion. There was a discrepancy in the high-end ready-to-wear fashion brands; cutting garments to fit slim bodies most black women do not have. After an emotional fitting-room experience, women would turn towards cheaper options that lack in quality. Olivia Rae Designs gives these same women the ability to strut in high quality, perfectly fitting, versatile garments. The brand introduces eye-catching pieces that are as unique as the women who wear them.

By age 19 Olivia Rae is utilizing seam-work and prints. This self-taught young designer wants women to be bold and stand out while wearing ORD garments. With a great focus on fit, Olivia Rae Designs maintains perfect tailoring and clean lines. Customers will own garments that make them feel powerful and confident while embracing their beauty because their garment was made especially for them.
